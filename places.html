FAMOUS TOURIST SPOTS IN THE PHILIPPINES

Banaue Rice Terraces - prime example of a developed, living cultural landscape that dates back as long as two millennia in the pre-colonial Philippines is the Rice Terraces of the Philippine Cordilleras. The terraces are situated on the northern island of Luzon in the Philippine archipelago's rugged Philippine Cordillera mountain region.

The geomorphic wonder and incredibly breathtaking Bohol Chocolate Hills. With its 1,268 cone-shaped hills, which turn chocolate brown during the dry season, it is one of the most amazing tourist destinations in the Philippines.

Boracay's 4-kilometer stretch of snow-white, powdery sand known as White Beach has long been praised as one of the best white sand beaches in the Philippines and the entire globe.
